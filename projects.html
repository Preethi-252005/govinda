Information about Project:

In my first year, I collaborated with my team on a project titled "Smart Home Automation" that utilised Internet of Things (IoT) components to develop a comprehensive solution integrating multiple applications. The project included:

  1. Smart Street Lighting: Operated using a PIR sensor for energy efficiency.
  2. Smart Irrigation System: Optimised water usage in agriculture.
  3. Smart Water Tank: Monitored and controlled water levels using an ultrasonic sensor.
  4. Smart Clothes Drying System: Used a rain sensor to protect clothes from rain.

We successfully completed the project with exceptional results, earning appreciation from our faculty, course coordinators, and KL officials. This accomplishment, early in my academic journey, was a proud moment that reinforced my passion for innovation and technology.

The smart home project mentioned above was exhibited at the IoT Expo and won the 3rd prize for its innovative idea and the team's hard work.
